What is OSM?

OpenStreetMap is a free editable map of the world for everyone to share and use. OSM powers the maps of notable applications such as Foursquare, Craigslist, and Evernote. Since 2004, the OSM community has attracted a user base of over one million and is currently experiencing unprecedented growth.

OSM map data is made available under the Open Data License.

Benefits

  • Free maps for your websites and mobile apps
  • Complete control of map design
  • Host anywhere -- on your own machine or others
  • Many quality commercial solutions available
